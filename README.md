## 📖 What is Novel-OS?

Novel-OS is a tool that uses multiple AI agents to help you write novels. It can remember parts of your story, check if your plot stays consistent, and manage an editorial process with 5 different agents working together. The software aims to support creative writing in a way that feels natural and organized.

This app runs on Windows. It guides you through writing novels while keeping track of characters, scenes, and storylines automatically. You don’t need to have any technical knowledge to use it.

## 🖥️ System Requirements

Before you download, make sure your computer meets these requirements:

- Windows 10 or later (64-bit)
- At least 4 GB of RAM
- 2 GHz processor or faster
- 500 MB of free hard drive space
- Internet connection recommended for setup and updates

If your machine meets these, Novel-OS should run smoothly.

## 🔧 Key Features

- **Multi-agent system:** Five AI agents handle different parts of the writing and editing process.
- **Persistent memory:** The app remembers everything you add, keeping your story consistent.
- **Automated continuity check:** It spots plot holes or inconsistencies automatically.
- **Editorial pipeline:** Each AI agent works on a different stage of your novel, from drafting to revising.
- **Easy export:** Save your work as text files you can open anywhere.
- **User-friendly interface:** Designed for writers, not programmers.

## ⚙️ How to Use Novel-OS

Novel-OS is designed to be intuitive. Here are the first things to do:

### Create a New Project

- Click **New Project** to start a fresh novel.
- Enter your novel's title.
- Optionally, add a short description.

### Add Characters

- Use the **Characters** section to create profiles.
- Add names, traits, and relationships.
- The AI will use this info to keep your story consistent.

### Write Scenes

- Open the **Scenes** tab to add parts of your story.
- The AI agents will help by suggesting text or checking flow.

### Use the Editorial Pipeline

- After writing, go to **Editorial Pipeline**.
- Here, the AI will check your work in stages:
  1. Draft review
  2. Continuity check
  3. Language polish
  4. Plot summary
  5. Final recommendations

- Review suggestions and accept or reject them.

### Save and Export

- Save your work automatically or manually.
- Export your novel as a plain text file or other formats offered in the app.

## 📂 Where Your Files Are Stored

Novel-OS saves your projects on your local drive.

- The default folder is `Documents\Novel-OS\Projects`.
- You can change this folder in the app settings.
- Back up your projects regularly by copying this folder to another location.
