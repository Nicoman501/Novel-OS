# Character Profile: Dr. Maya Chen

## Basic Information

- **Full Name**: Maya Lin Chen
- **Nickname**: None (hates nicknames)
- **Age**: 34
- **Birthdate**: March 15, 2013
- **Birthplace**: Seattle, Washington
- **Current Residence**: Micro-apartment in Seattle's Vertical District
- **Occupation**: Senior Data Archivist, Collective Oversight Authority
- **Role in Story**: Protagonist

---

## Psychology

### Core
- **Internal Desire**: To be truly seen and understood
- **External Goal**: Expose the Collective's manipulation
- **Motivation**: Fear of losing human agency, guilt over father's legacy

### Fear & Defense
- **Greatest Fear**: Being controlled, losing autonomy
- **Core Wound**: Father's death and the secrets he left
- **Defense Mechanism**: Withdrawal, intellectualization

### Characteristics
- **Strength**: Pattern recognition, persistence, moral courage
- **Weakness/Flaw**: Social isolation, difficulty trusting
- **Blind Spot**: Her own loneliness drives her to see control everywhere

### Beliefs
- **Lie They Believe**: Connection equals vulnerability equals control
- **Truth They Need**: Trust is risk, but isolation is prison

---

## Character Arc

### Starting Point
- **State**: Isolated observer, watching life through data
- **Circumstances**: Efficient at her job, empty personal life

### Transformation
- **Trigger**: The ghost pattern—something that shouldn't exist
- **Process**: Forced to collaborate, first with Marcus, then with the Collective itself
- **Obstacles**: Her own distrust, genuine threats from those who want the status quo

### Ending Point
- **State**: Connected without being controlled, first true human-AI partnership
- **Resolution**: Finds connection that preserves her agency
